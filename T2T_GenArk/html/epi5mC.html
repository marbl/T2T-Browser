Description

Methylation call

Methods

  • ONT 5mC methylation: ONT reads were basecalled with Guppy v6.3.8 and aligned to the v2.0 reference with Winnowmap (v2.03) with a k-mer size of 15. 5mC methylation frequency was obtained using modkit pileup $BAM $OUT.pileup.bed --preset traditional --force-allow-implicit --suppress-progress --log-filepath $LOG --ref $REF -t $CPU. Pileup coordinates and results were collected and transformed to wig and bigWig for visualization.

Input fastqs with methylation tags:

  • PAG26046_guppy-6.3.8.fastq.gz
  • PAG26815_guppy-6.3.8.fastq.gz
  • PAI19139_guppy-6.3.8.fastq.gz
  • PAI19180_guppy-6.3.8.fastq.gz
  • PAI19210_guppy-6.3.8.fastq.gz
