Description

Accessibility mask for T2T primate genome assemblies.

Method

Sequencing reads from each sample were aligned to its corresponding T2T primate reference assembly (primary haplotype). Then, we analyzed the CRAM files to determine normalized read depth, mapping quality, and base-calling quality. Following published protocols, the accessibility mask was created for each species. (Identifying genomic regions with high-quality single nucleotide variant calling).

Depth mask criteria:

High-Quality Bases: Defined as bases where the mean normalized coverage across samples fall within 25% of the median autosomal coverage.

Quality mask criteria:

Mapping Quality: Retain positions with a mapping quality of 50 or higher.
